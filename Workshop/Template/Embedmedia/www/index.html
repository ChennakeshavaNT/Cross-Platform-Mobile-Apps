iFrame is to be taken from the file origin host website
For Example: SoundClouds.com From Audio files
Steps to get I frame embed tag
1. Open SoundClouds.com and select your favourite song
2. Click the selected Song
3. Click share then choose Embed
4. check below for embed tag , Copy and paste it to ur HTML Document

For Example: Youtube.com From Video files
Steps to get I frame embed tag
1. Open youtube.com and select your favourite video
2. Click the selected video
3. Click share then choose Embed
4. check below for embed tag , Copy and paste it to ur HTML Document
